Our Goal

Mission:

To champion and nurture artists at all stages of their careers and to empower a diverse community through the presentation of inspiring exhibitions and educational initiatives.

Goals:

Leadership
Develop a team consisting of an effective and skilled Executive Board and staff equipped with optimal resources to further the mission of the gallery.
Program
Present challenging contemporary visual arts exhibitions and events, which enrich, educate and inspire a diverse community.
Facility
To establish a permanent facility in downtown Torrington which maintains the visibility and aesthetic standard of Five Points Gallery while allowing for growth and accessibility.
PR/Communications
To promote the awareness of the arts through consistent marketing and progressive communications with existing and potential artists, audiences and donors.
Finance
To build sustainable financial strength through major fundraising initiatives, grant writing, capital campaigns, donor cultivation and endowment planning.
